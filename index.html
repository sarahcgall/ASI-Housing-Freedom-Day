1. Calendar Chart

Status: IN PROGRESS
The base elements of the chart have been created and the chart itself has been made responsive (i.e., adjusts based on screen width) to ensure that tick labels do not become too small or overlap.

I have included a dropdown option for users to select their region (NB only England and Burnley are in there as an example). This will adjust the colours on the graph (red = payments to landlords; blue = freedom day). The dropdown is searchable for ease once the ~360 regions are added.

I have also added a calculator function for people to calculate their own freedom day based on their income and rent using: freedomDay = 1 + ((12 * rent) / income) * 365.25.
